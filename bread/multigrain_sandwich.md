# multigrain sandwich loaf (in progress)

Dave's Killer Bread recipes to try:
- [out of the box baking](https://outoftheboxbaking.com/2024/02/05/daves-bread-copycat-recipe/): same-day recipe, 45 min prep
- [serious eats](https://www.seriouseats.com/multigrain-sandwich-bread) (needs more seeds): 100% WW; soak the grains/seeds in parallel with 2.5 hr autolyse. assumes you have a food processor
- [fb/sourdoughgeeks first comment on post](https://www.facebook.com/groups/sourdoughgeeks/posts/9110195879076608/): 5 hrs bulk ferment, white/ww/rye blend
- [better baker club](https://betterbakerclub.com/daves-killer-bread-recipe/): 2 days. involves cooking wheat berries, making poolish, vital wheat gluten, finding/assembling multi grain mix and multi seed mix

## notes on [out of the box baking](https://outoftheboxbaking.com/2024/02/05/daves-bread-copycat-recipe/)
- 1 cup seeds (oats, sunflower, flax, sesame, poppy, chopped pistachios, pumpkin seeds, everything bagel seasoning, quinoa (rinsed))

- warm water, honey, yeast, 50/50 white/WW bread flour
- add salt, oats, seeds
- olive oil
- knead, log, coat with 1/2 cup more seeds
- let rise 1hr ish (it will not rise much
- bake in 9x5 loaf pan 375F 45-50min

## notes on [serious eats recipe](https://www.seriouseats.com/multigrain-sandwich-bread)

- seed mix: rolled oats 1oz, flaxseed / hemp hearts 1.5oz, chia 1oz, wheat germ 0.5oz. soak covered 2.5 hrs in 2oz water while waiting for WW flour to autolyse (in just water)
- bulk double (2 hrs at 70F)
- bake in 9x5 loaf pan 350F 45min, let cool completely

dough
- 15oz WW flour (about 3-1/3 cups)
- 11.25oz cool water
- 1.75oz brown sugar
- 3/8oz kosher salt
- 1/4oz instant dry yeast (not active dry)
- 1oz oil
