# multigrain sandwich loaf (in progress)

Dave's Killer Bread recipes to try:
- [out of the box baking](https://outoftheboxbaking.com/2024/02/05/daves-bread-copycat-recipe/): same-day recipe, 45 min prep
- [serious eats](https://www.seriouseats.com/multigrain-sandwich-bread) (needs more seeds): 100% WW; soak the grains/seeds in parallel with 2.5 hr autolyse. assumes you have a food processor
- [fb/sourdoughgeeks first comment on post](https://www.facebook.com/groups/sourdoughgeeks/posts/9110195879076608/): 5 hrs bulk ferment, white/ww/rye blend
- [better baker club](https://betterbakerclub.com/daves-killer-bread-recipe/): 2 days. involves cooking wheat berries, making poolish, vital wheat gluten, finding/assembling multi grain mix and multi seed mix
